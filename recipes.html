3-Ingredient Peppermint Bark

    Ingredients:

  • 1 bag (12 oz) Semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 bag (12 oz) White chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup crushed Candy Canes

    Instructions:

  1. Melt Dark Layer: Melt the semisweet chocolate in the microwave (30-second intervals, stirring in between). Spread it thinly onto a parchment-lined baking sheet. Freeze for 10 minutes.
  2. Melt White Layer: Melt the white chocolate the same way. Spread it gently over the hardened dark chocolate layer.
  3. Top & Chill: Immediately sprinkle the crushed candy canes on top. Refrigerate until completely hard (about 20 minutes), then break into pieces.

Caprese Christmas Wreath

    Ingredients:

  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Mini mozzarella balls
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze (for drizzling)
  • Toothpicks

    Instructions:

  1. Assemble: On a large platter, arrange the ingredients in a circle to look like a wreath. You can skewer them (Tomato -> Basil -> Mozzarella) or just lay them out flat.
  2. Garnish: Drizzle balsamic glaze over the circle just before serving.
  3. Serve: Place a small bowl of extra glaze or olive oil in the center of the wreath for dipping

    Ingredients:

  • 6 cups Chex cereal (Rice or Corn)
  • 1 cup Chocolate chips
  • 1 cup Chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup Peanut butter
  • 1 1/2 cups Powdered sugar
  • 1 cup Red and Green M&Ms

    Instructions:

  1. Melt: In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the chocolate chips and peanut butter together until smooth.
  2. Coat: Pour the mixture over the cereal in a large bowl and stir gently until coated.
  3. Shake: Pour the coated cereal into a large Ziploc bag. Add the powdered sugar, seal the bag, and shake until the cereal is white.
